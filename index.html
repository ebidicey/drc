About


The Plateau State Disability Rights Commission is an agency of the Plateau State Government. Plateau State is one of the 36 states in the Federal Republic of Nigeria. It is situated in the North-Central Region of Nigeria. Plateau State has a land mass of ... and a population of ... About ...% of this population are Persons with Disabilities (PWD).

The Plateau State Disability Rights Commission was established by the Plateau State Government to carryout among other functions, the following functions:
  • Make and implement policies, and activities aimed at full inclusion of persons with disabilities within the society.
  • Seek and create avenues for persons with disabilities to develop and utilise their talents and skills to become self-reliant.
  • Introduce programmes with an aim of promoting educational advancement and employment of persons with disabilities.
  • Make, implement and promote policies that encourage Persons with disabilities to participate in the social, economic land political life of the society.
  • Support the establishment and empowerment of disability organisations and public enlightenment
  • network with similar organisations that share same functions and views.
  • conduct and promote research and advocacy activities in Gallery areas of disability.

Mission


The mission of PLDRC is to build facilities and organise activities that ensure the full inclusion of persons with disabilities into the mainstream of the society, especially in the areas of education, employment, rehabilitation and civil rights.
